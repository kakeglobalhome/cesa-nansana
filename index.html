About Us

Who We Are

Centre For Special Abilities (CeSA) is a special needs centre located in Nansana, Uganda, offering Special Classes from Nursery through P1–S4. We work with children with special needs, supporting their development through assessment, therapy, education and vocational training.

Our work is guided by our vision, mission and motto:

Read our full Centre Profile →

Our Vision

A society in which children with special needs can grow and develop to their full potential for independent living.

Our Mission

To promote a God-fearing, equal rights and equitable opportunities for children with special needs through assessment, rehabilitation, capacity building and advocacy.

Our Motto

"Dare to be true." It's a daily commitment — to be honest with the children and families we serve, true to our mission, and unafraid to advocate for what every child deserves.

Our Objectives

What Guides Our Work

  1. To conduct community mobilisation for sensitisation, and child psycho-educational assessment for intervention.
  2. To promote children's health and advocate for their rights.
  3. To develop basic vocational skills in affordable and manageable fields like agriculture, music, dance & drama, food preparation and home management.
  4. To act as a model centre for hands-on training of students from higher institutions of learning.
  5. To promote environmental conservation strategies and practices for sustainable development.

What We Do

Our Programs & Services

CeSA offers a range of services to support children with special needs and their families.

  • Assessment & Counselling
  • Occupational Therapy
  • Speech & Language Therapy
  • Behaviour Management
  • Community-Based Rehabilitation (CBR)
  • Basic Vocational Training
  • Staff Capacity Building
  • Special Classes: Nursery – P1–S4

We also promote community awareness, children's health and rights, vocational skills in agriculture, music, dance & drama, and environmental conservation.
