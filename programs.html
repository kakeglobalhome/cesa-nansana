What We Do

Our Programs & Services

A range of integrated services designed to support the whole child — from first assessment through to vocational training.

🔍

Assessment & Counselling

Understanding each child is the first step to supporting them well.

Every child who comes to CeSA begins with a comprehensive psycho-educational assessment. This helps us understand their strengths, challenges, and individual learning profile. From there, we develop a personalised support plan — and revisit it regularly as the child grows.

Counselling is also available for children and their families, helping to process emotions, build resilience, and navigate the journey together.

🤲

Occupational Therapy

Building the everyday skills that make independent living possible.

Occupational therapy at CeSA focuses on helping children develop the practical skills they need for daily life — dressing, eating, writing, sensory regulation, and more. Sessions are hands-on and tailored to each child's goals.

Our therapists work closely with families to extend progress beyond the centre, supporting children in their home environments too.

💬

Speech & Language Therapy

Every child deserves to be heard and understood.

Speech and language therapy supports children who have difficulty speaking, understanding language, or communicating in other ways. This includes children who are non-verbal and use alternative forms of communication.

Our therapists use a range of approaches — play-based, structured, and aided communication — depending on the child's needs and age.

🌿

Behaviour Management

Gentle, structured support for self-regulation and positive habits.

Some children struggle with regulating their emotions or behaviour — not because they want to, but because they haven't yet developed the skills to do so. CeSA provides structured, compassionate support to help children build self-awareness, manage their reactions, and develop positive behaviour patterns.

We work with teachers, families and children together, ensuring consistency across school and home.

🏘️

Community-Based Rehabilitation

Taking support beyond the centre, into families and communities.

CBR is about recognising that a child's development happens everywhere — not just at school. CeSA works with families in their homes and communities, building the capacity of parents and caregivers to support their children's growth.

We also engage local leaders and community members to raise awareness and reduce the stigma that too many children with disabilities still face in Uganda.

🌱

Basic Vocational Training

Practical skills that open doors to economic independence.

CeSA offers functional vocational training in areas such as agriculture, cooking, crafts, music, and home management. These are skills that children can build on throughout their lives — and that can support them in earning a livelihood in adulthood.

Training is hands-on, adapted to each learner's ability level, and designed to build confidence alongside practical competence.

📈

Staff Capacity Building

Better-trained staff means better outcomes for every child.

CeSA is committed to the ongoing professional development of its teachers, therapists and support staff. This includes in-house training, external workshops, and collaboration with higher education institutions.

We also serve as a placement centre for students from universities and training colleges, providing hands-on experience in special needs education and therapy.

📚

Special Classes: Nursery – Senior Four

Structured learning adapted to every child's level and pace.

CeSA runs full academic classes from Nursery level through to Senior Four (roughly equivalent to GCSE level). Curriculum is adapted to each child's learning needs, with small class sizes and individualised support.

We follow the Ugandan national curriculum where possible, while making the modifications each child needs to access learning meaningfully — because academic progress, at any pace, matters.
